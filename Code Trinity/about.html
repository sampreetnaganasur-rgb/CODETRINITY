Our Legacy

Building Excellence Since 1946

A Journey of 75+ Years

B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE) was founded in the year 1946 by Late Sri. B. M. Sreenivasaiah, a great visionary and philanthropist. It was the first private sector initiative in engineering education in India.

Over the decades, the institution has produced world-class engineers who are leading global organizations today. We are proud to be an institution that blends traditional values with modern technology.

"Providing quality education is the highest form of service to society."

— Late Sri. B. M. Sreenivasaiah, Founder
