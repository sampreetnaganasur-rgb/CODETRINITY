Academic Focus Areas

  • Aerodynamics: Analyzing the interaction of air with moving bodies.
  • Flight Mechanics: Studying the stability and control of aircraft during maneuvers.
  • Aero-Structures: Designing lightweight, high-strength frames for air and space vehicles.
  • Astronautics: Understanding orbital mechanics and satellite launch systems.
